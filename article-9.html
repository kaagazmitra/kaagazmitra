How to Register for an e-Shram Card in 2025 – Complete Process

Published on July 08, 2025

The e-Shram card is a government initiative to create a national database of unorganized workers. This card provides access to various social security schemes. The registration process is simple and can be done online. If you need any help, Kaagaz Mitra is available to assist you.

Who Can Register for an e-Shram Card?

Any unorganized worker between the ages of 16-59 is eligible. This includes construction workers, migrant laborers, street vendors, domestic workers, and agricultural laborers who are not members of EPFO/ESIC or an income taxpayer.

Step-by-Step Registration Process

Step 1: Visit the e-Shram Portal

Go to the official e-Shram portal (eshram.gov.in). The self-registration requires your Aadhaar number and a mobile number linked to it.

Step 2: Aadhaar Authentication

Enter your Aadhaar-linked mobile number and the captcha. You will receive an OTP for verification. After entering the OTP, you'll be asked for your Aadhaar number for another OTP-based authentication.

Step 3: Fill in Your Personal and Occupational Details

Once authenticated, your demographic details from Aadhaar will be displayed. You will need to fill in additional information, including your address, educational qualifications, occupation, and bank account details. Providing accurate bank details is crucial for receiving direct benefit transfers (DBT).

Step 4: Download Your e-Shram Card

After submitting all the required information, your e-Shram card will be generated. It will have a 12-digit Universal Account Number (UAN). You can download the card and save it for your records.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there any fee for e-Shram registration?

No, the registration for the e-Shram card is completely free of cost.

What are the benefits of having an e-Shram card?

The e-Shram card gives you access to various social security schemes, including insurance benefits under PMSBY (Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana), and helps in the delivery of other welfare schemes.

What if my mobile number is not linked to my Aadhaar?

If your mobile number is not linked to your Aadhaar, you can visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) to complete the registration process using their biometric authentication device.
