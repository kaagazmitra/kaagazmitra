How to Apply for a Passport from Home: A Complete 2025 Guide

Published on July 08, 2025

Applying for a passport can seem like a daunting task, but thanks to the online services provided by the Government of India, it's now easier than ever to complete the process from the comfort of your home. This guide will walk you through the essential steps. Of course, if you need help at any stage, your Digital Saathi at Kaagaz Mitra is just a call away.

Phase 1: Online Preparation

Step 1: Registration on the Passport Seva Portal

The very first step is to register on the official Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) website. You'll need a valid email ID to create an account. During registration, you'll be asked to choose the passport office you wish to apply to based on your current residential address.

Step 2: Filling the Application Form (Form 49A)

Once registered, log in to your account and click on the "Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue of Passport" link. You will have two options: Fill the form online or download and fill the e-Form. We recommend filling the form online. Ensure all details like your name, parents' names, and date of birth are entered exactly as they appear on your official documents.

Step 3: Document Upload and Fee Payment

After filling the form, you'll need to upload scanned copies of your required documents. These typically include proof of address (like an Aadhaar card or utility bill) and proof of date of birth (like a birth certificate or 10th-grade mark sheet). The portal will then direct you to the payment gateway to pay the application fees online. Payment is mandatory to book an appointment.

Phase 2: In-Person Verification

Step 4: Scheduling and Attending Your Appointment

Once the payment is successful, you can schedule an appointment at your nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK). You must visit the center on the scheduled date with your original documents for verification. The officials will check your documents, take your photograph, and capture your biometrics (fingerprints).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long does it take to get a passport?

Typically, a passport is dispatched within 30 days of the appointment under normal processing. Tatkaal services are faster but cost more.

What is the fee for a fresh passport?

As of 2025, the fee for a fresh passport with a 36-page booklet is ₹1500. Fees are subject to change, so always check the official portal.

Do I need to submit original documents?

Yes, you must carry all your original documents to the PSK for verification during your appointment. They will be returned to you after verification.
