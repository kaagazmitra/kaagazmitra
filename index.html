About Kaagaz Mitra

Kaagaz Mitra was born from a simple idea: to make life easier for everyone in India. We saw how time-consuming and confusing dealing with paperwork and daily chores can be. Our mission is to provide a reliable, friendly, and digital-first personal assistance service that you can access from the comfort of your home.

Our team is dedicated to providing end-to-end support, guiding you through every step of the process. We believe in building trust through transparency and efficiency.
