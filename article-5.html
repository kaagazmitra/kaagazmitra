How to Apply for an Aadhaar Card for the First Time in 2025

Published on July 08, 2025

The Aadhaar card is a 12-digit unique identity number that has become one of the most important documents for Indian citizens. Applying for it for the first time is a straightforward process that must be done in person. If you need help booking an appointment or preparing your documents, Kaagaz Mitra is here to guide you.

Phase 1: Booking Your Appointment

Step 1: Locate an Enrolment Centre

You can find your nearest Aadhaar Enrolment Centre through the official UIDAI website. It's recommended to book an appointment online to avoid long queues.

Step 2: Fill the Enrolment Form

Download the Aadhaar Enrolment/Correction Form from the UIDAI website and fill it out completely. You will need to provide basic demographic information like your name, address, date of birth, gender, and mobile number.

Phase 2: The Enrolment Process

Step 3: Visit the Enrolment Centre

On the day of your appointment, visit the centre with your filled form and original supporting documents. You will need Proof of Identity (PoI), Proof of Address (PoA), and Proof of Date of Birth (DoB).

Step 4: Biometric Data Collection

At the centre, an operator will take your photograph, fingerprints of all ten fingers, and two iris scans. This biometric data is unique to you and is a key security feature of Aadhaar.

Step 5: Collect the Acknowledgment Slip

After the process is complete, you will receive an acknowledgment slip containing your 14-digit Enrolment ID (EID). You can use this EID to track the status of your Aadhaar application online.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is there any fee for Aadhaar enrolment?

No, Aadhaar enrolment for the first time is completely free of cost for all residents.

How long does it take to get an Aadhaar card?

It can take up to 90 days for your Aadhaar to be generated and delivered to your address after enrolment. However, you can download the e-Aadhaar from the UIDAI website once it is generated.

Can I apply for an Aadhaar card online?

No, you cannot apply for a new Aadhaar card completely online. You must visit an enrolment centre in person for the collection of your biometric data.
